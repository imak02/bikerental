We, at Fusion Bike Rental Services, are into two wheeler rental operation since a long time. We are the pioneer in this service in Lalitpur. As per present scenario of heavy traffic jam and people running short of time, it is important to have easy mode of transportation and more importantly to have easy access of this mode of transport. When we thought about this problem, we started surveying people coming from different background and different locations. We found that two wheeler is easy to handle and park, fuel efficient and easy to move anywhere across the city. We started our journey with just two vehicles. Currently we are managing a fleet of plenty vehicles and a lot of customers, and the journey is going on. We are moving towards a new height with trust of our esteemed customers.
